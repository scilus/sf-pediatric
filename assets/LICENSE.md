## License for Pediatric Brain Atlases

This directory contains pediatric white matter bundle atlases used for bundle segmentation and tractometry analysis across different developmental stages. It also include brain templates used for tissue segmentation.

### Atlas Collections

The following atlas collections are included:

-   **atlas-Infant00**: Neonatal/0-month infant atlas
-   **atlas-Infant03**: 3-month infant atlas
-   **atlas-Infant06**: 6-month infant atlas
-   **atlas-Infant12**: 12-month infant atlas
-   **atlas-Infant24**: 24-month infant atlas
-   **atlas-Children**: Pediatric children atlas
